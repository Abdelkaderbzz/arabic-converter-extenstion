The problem it solves

Writing Arabic should not depend on having the right keyboard layout, or slowing down to hunt for Arabic letters.

⌨️

No Arabic keyboard?

You want to write in Fusha or Tunisian dialect but only have a Latin keyboard. Type naturally in Roman letters and get correct Arabic script in seconds.

⚡

Prefer Latin / Arabizi?

You can use an Arabic keyboard, but Arabizi is faster for you: 3aslema, 7alek, 9rib. Convert to polished Arabic without retyping every word.

🌐

Need it while you work?

Messages, posts, emails, notes. Open the extension popup, paste or type, copy the result, and keep going. No tab switching or separate app required.

What you get

✓

Fusha & Tunisian modes

Switch between Modern Standard Arabic and Tunisian Arabic script with one tap.

✓

Smart Arabizi mapping

Understands number substitutions like 3 → ع, 7 → ح, 9 → ق, and more.

✓

English & Arabic UI

Full bilingual interface with RTL support.

✓

Copy, history & bookmarks

Copy output instantly, revisit recent conversions, and save favorites with bookmarks.

✓

Powered by Google Gemini

Accurate AI conversion using your own free Gemini API key, stored locally in your browser.

✓

Private by design

Your API key and history stay on your device. Text is sent only to Google Gemini for conversion.

How it works

1

Install the extension

Add Arabic Converter from the Chrome Web Store and pin it to your toolbar.

2

Add your Gemini key

Open settings, paste a free key from Google AI Studio, and save. One-time setup.

3

Type, convert, copy

Write in Latin, choose Fusha or Tunisian, hit Convert, and paste the Arabic result anywhere.
