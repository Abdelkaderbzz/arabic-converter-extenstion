## English version

🎉 **Arabizzi is now officially published on Mortakaz**

I'm excited to share that **Arabizzi** — my free Chrome extension for converting Arabizi and Latin text to Arabic — is now live on **Mortakaz**, the Arabic platform for discovering and supporting tech projects.

What it does:
• Convert Arabizi → colloquial Arabic or MSA (Fusha)
• Translate to English or French
• Bilingual UI, conversion history, and Gemini-powered settings

🔗 arabizzi.com

If you've tried it or find it useful, your support on Mortakaz means a lot.
